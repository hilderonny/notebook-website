PWA demo with background notification on Android

Version 46

At the very beginning install the PWA on your phone by clicking on the hint which appears on the bottom in chrome on Android.

Next start the installed application. You will get promted for the permission to send notifications. Confirm it.

Next click on "Trigger notification after 5 seconds" and close the application window by pressing the Home button. You can optionally turn your phone into standby mode by switching the diasplay off. After 5 seconds a system notification will appear (I hope so).

The notification permission can be removed in chrome://settings/content/notifications.
